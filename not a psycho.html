How it came to be

I started drawing a lot of knives because they were fun to draw. It adds an eerie and offputting aura to drawings, especially if they aren't expected and they're also shiny. I kept drawing knives...they were taking long to get out of my system. My friends held an intervention, which was funny and wholesome. This poster is a response that (sorry guys!).

Design inspiration: American Psycho

The movie poster designs for Amerian Psycho are so so sick (both official and unofficial, check out some of my favs here). I thought that the iconic movie poster (right) was such a perfect concept for this poster, so I ran with it. It was pretty embarrassing to take the reference photos for this poster, so please ooh and aaah and appreciate the turmoil I went through to make this.

A mid-process shot, and some general vibes.

The "Knifey Materials Production" is a running bit throughout the posters, obviously inspired by this one. It's really funny to me (and probably just me).

This was the very first poster that I made, so it has a special place for me. It was also I felt the most challenging one to design. One, because I hadn't done much visual design in a very long time so I was dusting off the metaphorical cobwebs. Two, there were a lot of elements I wanted to include, and a lot of photo editing required for this (thanks Photoshop).
