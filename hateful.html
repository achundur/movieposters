All's fair in love and Mario Kart.

How it came to be

I finally beat my friend in Mario Kart; the winner earns the right to gloat like a tyrannical king.

Design inspiration: westerns

It's like The Hateful Eight except it's one person. I've been loving the look of old tintype photographs, from the early years of photography. This poster was probably the most fun to edit. Who knew hoarding my old Kohl's poncho from 8th grade would finally come in useful. My trusty impulse-purchase cowboy hat was perfect, as per usual.
