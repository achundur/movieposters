Friendly but punk

Gratuitously ominous.

How it came to be

The monologue in the poster text pretty much says it all, but I bought a fantastic Toad graphic t-shirt off of an illustrator's kickstarter campaign. My friend said that the photos of me in the shirt had a 'mildly threatening aura' and fit my 'friendly but punk attitude' (an extremely wonderful compliment). Thus, this poster was born.

The shirt in question, by @abcxyz on Instagram

Design inspiration: Swiss design, alternative, and cyberpunk

Again, check poster monologue, but at this point in time, I was super into neon, retina burning colors. I'd also recently watched the show Cyberpunk: Edgerunners (which is fantastic btw), and the opening sequence has this really nice bright, RGB yellow with bold typography.
In terms of composition I really wanted to get funky with it and break some (all) rules. I took inspiration from Swiss poster design (with the nice sans-serif typography) and alternative, futuristic-esque designs (for the composition).

Inspo posters, and some general vibes.
