About

This project started as a way to occupy myself in the depths of winter. I love to keep a list of the funniest quotes and moments from my friends. I noticed that there was a growing collection of (very funny) bits about me. One day, I just had the idea to make something out of it- I had been wanting to dive back into visual design for a while at that point, and the idea to make movie posters just popped into my head.
I love this project. The quotes are so funny and it reminds me of great memories. The posters were challenging but rewarding to make, and I feel I'm back to loving and appreciating visual design a lot more. While this is also kind of embarrassing (the mortifying ordeal of being seen trying -_-) I think sometimes its better to be shameless and create things that push you creatively and make you excited.
