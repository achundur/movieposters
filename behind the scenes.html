Fun facts:

  • All of the photos were shot in my room! I made my room into a little photo studio with white sheets, curtains, and sometimes my sunset lamp
  • The "American Psycho" and "Hateful One" posters are actually composite images- I photoshopped my head and body together from different images to get the right positioning I wanted!
  • Building the mini sets, getting in costume, remote shooting with my phone, figuring out if I was framed properly and the image was focused while a bright light was shining in my face made me appreciate the work that goes into photoshoots...and why they have fans to cool the room down.
