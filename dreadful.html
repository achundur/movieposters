How it came to be

I was making dumb faces on zoom while my friend wasn't looking. When she saw me, she shouted "ugh, you look dreadful like that!"

Design inspiration: A24, horror

While rooting around for inspiration, I came across some really wonderful horror movie posters. In particular, I loved the vintage aesthetic and striking and unique imagery. I'd also really been wanting to incorporate a Blackletter typeface into one of the posters, and this seemed perfect.

Inspo posters, and some general vibes.

More design details

  • Main title typeface: SchwarzKopf New
  • Text: Times New Roman Bold
  • Tools used: Lightroom, Photoshop, Indesign
