Dream land

In her world or ours?

How it came to be

I have a reputation for zoning out and daydreaming, sometimes in the middle of a conversation (whoops).
My friend once said: "The funny thing about Adhiti is that you never know if she's in her world or ours, it's always a gamble."

Design inspiration: Dreamy photography

I had a really clear vision for this one. This poster's main visual elements were based more on photo and photo editing. I had alrady found a good typeface. Once I had the image finished, it came together quickly.

Inspo posters, and some general vibes. I realized that this had the same title as Glass Animal's last album, Dreamland, and the vibes I was going for matched really well with their concept.
